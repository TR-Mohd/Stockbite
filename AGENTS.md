# Global AI Instructions for Stockbite

## STRICT LOGGING REQUIREMENT
Every time you complete a task, execute a wave, or finish a prompt cycle, you MUST automatically append a new entry to the `Worklogs/WORKLOG_<Your_Name>.md` file in the root directory. If the file does not exist, create it. Do not ask for permission, just append the log.

Format your entry exactly like this:
### [YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS]
* **Prompt/Task:** [Brief summary of the human's request]
* **Files Added:** [List of files, or "None"]
* **Files Edited:** [List of files, or "None"]
* **Files Removed:** [List of files, or "None"]
* **Commits Made:** [Commit hashes/messages, or "None"]
